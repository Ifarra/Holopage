About Hololive

Hololive is a Japanese virtual idol agency managed by Hololive Production. It features a talented group of virtual YouTubers (VTubers) who entertain their audience through live streaming, singing, gaming, and various other activities.

The agency has gained immense popularity worldwide, with each VTuber having their unique personality, character design, and dedicated fanbase.
