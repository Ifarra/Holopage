Kureiji Ollie of hololive Indonesia Releases a New Visual Novel “Days with Ollie” for PC

COVER Corporation (Minato-ku, Tokyo; CEO: Tanigo Motoaki) has announced that the VTuber Kureiji Ollie of “hololive Indonesia” will release a new visual novel game “Days with Ollie” for PC. Please enjoy the world of “Days with Ollie” produced by Kureiji Ollie!

Story
After so much waiting, the day has finally come. Interview day. The day that will determine whether or not you’ll be accepted as a reporter in hololive. And not just any reporter, but the reporter for the amazing and talented Kureiji Ollie! Wonder what goes down in the life of the zombie idol? Find out what it’ll be like to spend Days with Ollie!
